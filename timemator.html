Flexible task structure

Do you have projects and tasks? Or maybe clients, their projects, tasks, and sub-tasks? Or just simple trackable items? It doesn't matter: Timemator supports any structure you define. Just use its folders and tasks as you do in the Finder.

Menu bar display
The timer gets out of your way and always stays just a click away in the menu bar. You can also have a quick glance to check how much time you already spent without losing focus. Configure what you want to see inside the menu bar. Choose from a variety of presets and select the right one for you.

Works completely offline

Timemator works everywhere. It doesn't matter if you are in an office or on an airplane. You will never lose a second just because there is no internet connection.

Privacy
What happens on your computer stays on your computer.
Timemator keeps all your private data like tracked sessions or revenue on your device and doesn't send them anywhere.
Automatic backups

Never worry about losing your data. Timemator creates automatic backups and equips you with the control to recover any previous data. You can also define your custom location like Dropbox, Google Drive or iCloud Drive.

Handcrafted Native UI
Timemator is a true Mac-app and uses native technologies to provide the best performance and user experience on your system.
